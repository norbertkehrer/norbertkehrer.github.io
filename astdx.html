AstDX and Ast3DX

AstDX, The Asteroids Emulator with New Graphics and Sound

AstDX is an emulator for Atari's Asteroids arcade machine with newly designed graphics and sound. It uses Atari's program ROMs from 1979, but instead of drawing vector graphics as in the original game, all game objects are replaced by animated sprites. In the original arcade machine the sound is made with special circuits for each sound effect. In the emulator this is achieved with digital samples (.wav files). I added also a background music track with 10 different songs in MIDI format.
AstDX runs under DOS.

Ast3DX, The 3D Asteroids Emulator

Ast3DX is an Asteroids emulator, which emulates Atari's Asteroids arcade machine, except that you play the game from the perspective of the cockpit of Asteroid's space ship. That means that Ast3DX is a first person 3D shooter with texture-mapped polygon objects, using the absolutely original Atari game play from 1979.
I added a background music track with 10 different songs from ABBA in MIDI format.
Ast3DX runs under DOS.

Download ast3dx.zip (989 kB).
