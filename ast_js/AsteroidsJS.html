Asteroids in JavaScript

Original game by Atari Inc. in 1979.

Exact port of the original to JavaScript using the method of static binary translation in October 2005 and in June 2014 by Norbert Kehrer.

Read more about it on my homepage at http://members.aon.at/~nkehrer/

Keys to play:
Rotate Left Crsr Left, Z, or Y
Rotate Right Right or X
Fire Ctrl or M
Thrust Up or N
Hyperspace Space
Start 1 Player 1
Start 2 Players 2
Insert Coin 5, or 6, or 7
