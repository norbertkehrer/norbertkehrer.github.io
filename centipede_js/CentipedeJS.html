Centipede in JavaScript

Original game by Atari in 1980.

Exact port of the original to JavaScript using the method of static binary translation in April 2015 by Norbert Kehrer.

Read more about it on my homepage at https://norbertkehrer.github.io

Keys to play:
Left Crsr Left or A
Right Crsr Right or D
Up Crsr Up or W
Down Crsr Down or S
Fire Ctrl or Space
Start 1/2 Players 1 or 2
Pause Game P
